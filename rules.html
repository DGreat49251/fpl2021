SCORING SYSTEM

  • Score per match = Rank points + 1% of the points scored in DREAM11 + 3 points if your team has more members from the winning team.
  • Rank points are points awarded on the basis of DREAM11 rank in the contest.
    • RANKPOINTS AWARDED
    First15
    Second10
    Third5
    Fourth1
    Fifth0
    Sixth0
    Seventh-1
    Eighth-3
    Ninth-5
    Tenth-8
  • Leg score = (total points scored by you/sum of points scored by all players ) * 100
  • Top two of each leg will be decided based on leg score.
  • After two legs: Top 4 will be selected according to the final leg score of the selected candidates after consideration of conditions listed above and below.

GENERAL RULES

  • Contest link will be sent by the scorer.
  • Rules can be added or modified on the will of the organisers.
  • The one who wins the final match will be the winner of this tournament.
  • It is permissible that at worst cases the contest link will be sent 15 minutes before the starting of the match so all the participants are advised to keep their teams ready beforehand so that they can join quickly.
  • For not joining the contest but making the team:-
    1. Reasons like net issues, device problems or website or app problems will not be entertained under any circumstances and will lead to absence.
    2. First day no issues it should be a warning, but no pts will be awarded.
    3. Second consecutive day highest score scored by the participant till date will be deducted once.
    4. Third consecutive day the same score will be deducted as on the second day.
    5. Fourth consecutive day the participant will be disqualified from the ongoing leg.

      6. NOTE: If anything serious occurs then it may be considered for non-disqualification only. But he will be facing deduction of points.

    6. If you have made the team but not joined the contest, then that participation will be considered null and void.
